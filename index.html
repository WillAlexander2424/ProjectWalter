Walter AI Advisory

Review
Lease Term Structure — Consider 5+2 instead of 6 years

The current 6-year initial term with 2x2 renewals (6+2+2) is unusual for this property class. Based on 847 comparable industrial leases in South Auckland, the market standard is 3+3+3 or 5+2. A 5+2 structure provides the tenant with a shorter initial commitment and a meaningful renewal option, while giving the landlord more frequent rent review opportunities.

Review
CPI / Rent Increase — 3% fixed is above forecast

The fixed 3% annual increase exceeds the RBNZ CPI forecast of 2.1-2.4% for 2026-2028. Comparable leases in Manukau average 2.5% fixed or CPI + 0.5%. Recommend negotiating to CPI-linked reviews capped at 3% to protect the tenant from overexposure in a softening inflation environment.

Good
$/sqm Rate — In line with market

At $290/sqm, this lease is within the $265-$310 range for Level 2 industrial/office space in the Osterley Way precinct. No adjustment recommended.

Alert
OPEX Apportionment — Tenant exposure may be high

The lease includes 13 OPEX categories apportioned to the tenant including structural repairs to fences, car park maintenance, and body corporate charges. Based on comparable properties, structural repair costs should be landlord responsibility. Recommend excluding items 7 (structural fencing) and 10 (repaving/resealing) from tenant OPEX. Estimated saving: $8-12/sqm p.a.

Review
Renewal Notice Period — Not specified

Field 7 (Renewal Notice Period) is blank in the First Schedule. Under ADLS 6th Edition clause 34.1, this defaults to 3 months. Consider explicitly setting this to 6 months to give both parties adequate planning time.

Good
Bond Amount — Standard

Bond of $30,477.82 equals approximately 3 months rent + GST, which is standard practice under ADLS guidelines.
